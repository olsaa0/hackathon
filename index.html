Robotics Milestones Timeline

1921

First Robot Play "R.U.R."

“Rossum’s Universal Robots” introduced the word "robot" to the world.

1961

Unimate Industrial Robot

The first industrial robot arm to work on a production line was introduced.

1997

Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov

IBM’s Deep Blue became the first computer chess system to defeat a reigning world champion.

2000s

Rise of Autonomous Robots

Robots like Roomba and Boston Dynamics’ Spot begin operating autonomously in homes and outdoors.

2020

Robotics in Healthcare & AI

Robots assist in surgeries, delivery, and AI-powered robotics grow rapidly.

Featured Robots

ASIMO

A humanoid robot developed by Honda known for its walking and running ability.

Lumino was designed to help people with limited mobility and perform basic tasks. It can navigate environments and interact with humans.

Cybrix

A quadruped robot capable of navigating rough terrain and inspecting hazardous areas.

Spot is used in construction, public safety, and even art installations. It can carry sensors and climb stairs.

Synthia

Used for minimally invasive surgeries, controlled by a surgeon with extreme precision.

It enhances the surgeon’s capabilities with robotic arms and HD cameras to perform delicate procedures with less trauma.
